Kinds of birds

Eagles

Eagles are predetory birds that hunt smaller birds, mammals and other types of animals to feed themselves. Eagles mainly live near large bodies of water like lakes, rivers or coastlines.

Parrots

Parrots are oppurtunistic omnivores that mainly feed on nuts and vegetation in their natural habitat. They have strong and sturdy beaks that they use to be able to open tough shells and hunt small insects.

Crows

Crows are omnivores that feed on a wide range of different things. They are known to be one of the most inteligent birds on Earth with the ability to use simple tools and conprehend simple logic.
