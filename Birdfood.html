Diets of Birds

Parakeet

Parkeets are a common type of bird that mainly feeds on pellets, seeds and fresh vegetable or fruits ocacionaly. They have a life span of around 5-8 years and make great pets.

Finches

Finches are another type of common bird pet found in America, Eurasia and parts of Africa. They mainly feed on pellets, seeds and leafy greens. They live for around 5-10 years

Lovebirds

Lovebirds mainly feed on pellets just like the other two kinds of pet birds but they requires more amount of vegetable and fruits.
